Registration Rules

Real on Reels / Reel on Vaishnav

Rules and regulations

 

  • Registrations can be done online only. There is no other mode for registration.
  • After Registration you will get a confirmation mail, if you don’t receive mail within 24 hours of registration please contact NAVARACHANAA team.
  • You are required to upload your documentary film on YouTube. The video uploaded on YouTube should not be public. The link to your YouTube video must be email at realonreel2019@gmail.comwith Registration ID in the subject line.
  • Total running time must be less than 5 minutes in length, including front and back credits. There is no minimum running time.
  • Competition is open to students from all courses.
  • The film must be based on one of the given themes only.
  • Multiple submissions per team are allowed but separate registration will be required for each individual entry.
  • Documentary film must be in Hindi or English language only.
  • Your film should be to the point and precise. Please do not plagiarize with additional data. Do not deviate from your central idea.
  • Including any kind of promotional material is prohibited.
  • Organization has right to use the entries submitted, DVD and materials from the documentary film for event promotional purposes and to retain DVD copies of each documentary film as part of our event library.
  • Ensure that all content in your film (including footage, music, images, props, etc.) is your own. If you include any copyrighted content, you must be able to provide written permission for its use.
  • All sources must be cited and credited in the annotated bibliography as well as in the credits of the documentary itself.
  • Shortlisted entries will be notified by mail and/or email after 28thFebruary, 2019.
  • The screening panel will upload your video after 28thFebruary, 2019 if your film gets selected in the screening round.
  • There will be a 2 minute judges’ cross questioning session immediately after the presentation of your film.
  • One DVD need to be submitted along with the entry on the date of event to the Event coordinators. Each Preview DVD should be labeled with the Title, Running-time, Contact E-mail and Phone number.
  • Participation certificates will be provided to all the participants.
  • Entries once submitted for selection will be considered as final and no changes will be entertained after submission. It is the sole responsibility of the applicant to email the documentary film without any quality defects.
  • Participation certificates will be given to registered team members only but you can include others for casting purpose if required in your documentary film.
